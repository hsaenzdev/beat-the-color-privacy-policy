Privacy Policy for Beat the Color

Last Updated: April 11, 2025

Overview

Beat the Color is a simple color-matching game that respects your privacy. We believe in being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection and Storage

1. Game Progress

  • We only store your high score locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences.
  • This data never leaves your device.
  • You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app's data through your device's settings.

2. Device Capabilities

  • The game checks basic device capabilities (like processor cores and vibration support) to optimize performance.
  • This information is only used during gameplay and is never stored or transmitted.

3. No Personal Information

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This includes but is not limited to your name, email address, or any online identifiers.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools to gather information about your usage of the game.
  • We do not require any user accounts or login to play Beat the Color.
  • We do not collect device identifiers such as IMEI, MAC address, or Android Advertising ID.

4. Permissions

  • The only permission we request is android.permission.VIBRATE, which is used solely for providing haptic feedback during gameplay.
  • No other permissions are required or requested by Beat the Color.

Data Sharing

  • We do not share any data with third parties because Beat the Color does not collect any personal or usage data that could be shared.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be modified accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
